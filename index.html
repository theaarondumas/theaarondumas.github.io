Frequently Asked Questions

Still unsure? Here are answers to the most common questions about LeanCut Coach and the programs.

Do I need access to a gym?
A gym is ideal, but modified home options are available. If you have dumbbells, bands, or basic equipment, you’ll be able to follow the plan.
Can beginners use these programs?
Yes. The structure works for beginners and intermediates. Beginners should use lighter weights, focus on form, and progress gradually.
How quickly will I see results?
Many people notice changes in energy, bloating, and the way clothes fit within 7–10 days. Deeper visual changes often show up by weeks 3–4.
What if I have a busy schedule?
The programs are designed for 3–5 hours of training per week. You’ll know exactly what to do when you walk into the gym — no wasted time.
Can I still eat out or have drinks?
Yes. We focus on hitting weekly calorie and protein targets first. You can fit in meals out or occasional drinks as long as you stay within your overall plan.
Is this a subscription?
The 4-Week Cut and 8-Week Shred are one-time purchases. Coaching is month-to-month with no long-term contracts.
